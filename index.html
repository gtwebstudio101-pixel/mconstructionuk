What We Do

Our Services

We specialise in all building projects, offering a complete package for any construction work in your home or workplace at highly competitive rates.

Extensions

We provide a design service for all home improvement projects, regardless of size or budget. Whether it's a house extension, loft conversion, or renovation, we deliver cost-effective solutions on time and on budget.

Basements

Basement conversions add value and create new living space. We assess water tables, substrate, and intended use to recommend the best system. We can transform an existing cellar or build entirely new space from existing foundations, with underpinning inspected by building control.

Bathrooms & Kitchens

We specialise in luxury bathroom and kitchen design and installation across London. Our experienced team handles every renovation from start to finish.

Gas Work: All gas work must by law be carried out by a CORGI-registered installer. We are fully registered with the Council for Registered Gas Installers, the body authorised by the Health & Safety Executive.

  • Plumbing
  • CORGI-registered Gas Work
