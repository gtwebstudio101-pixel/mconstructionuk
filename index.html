About Us

Quality Builders in London

Based on Orchardson Street, NW8, we are a trusted family of time-served builders offering comprehensive construction services across North West London and beyond. Every project — from a simple bathroom renovation to a full new build — is handled with the same care and attention to detail.

Our company philosophy is built on customer satisfaction, clean workmanship, and transparent communication from start to finish.

  • Wide range of services under one roof

What We Do

Our Services

We specialise in all building projects, offering a complete package for any construction work in your home or workplace at highly competitive rates.

Extensions

House extensions, loft conversions and home improvement projects designed on time and on budget — whatever the size or budget.

Roofing

All roofing work carried out with minimal disruption and backed by an insurance-backed guarantee.

  • Commercial & Domestic Roofing
  • Flat Roof Repairs & Felt Roofing
  • Re-Roofs & Roof Conversions
  • Leadwork & Storm Damage
Basements

Converting your basement into habitable space adds real value to your home. We assess, design and build from existing cellars or new foundations.

Bathrooms & Kitchens

Luxury bathroom design and installation. Our trusted team handles your renovation from start to finish, with CORGI-registered gas work.

Electrics

Domestic, commercial and industrial electrical installations.

  • Home Entertainment Systems
  • Security & Alarm Systems
  • Automatic Gates & Satellite
Landscaping & Decorating

  • Decking & Land Drainage
